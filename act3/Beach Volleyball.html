Petron XCS dug deep into its well of experience to pull off a 21-17, 21-16 victory over F2 Logistics in the quarterfinals of the 2016
Philippine Superliga (PSL) Challenge Cup beach volleyball tournament Sunday at the SM By the Bay in Mall of Asia. Aiza Maizo-Pontillas
and Bang Pineda displayed their spectacular form right from the opening serve to frustrate Aby Marano and Danika Gendrauli and coast to the semifinals.
